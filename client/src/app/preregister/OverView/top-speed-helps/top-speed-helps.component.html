How TopSpeed Helps

TopSpeed helps throughout the learning process
  • An instructor may prepare a Programmed Learning lecture for students.
  • Tags are used to navigate the presentation based on the student's understanding.
  • Other tags prompt a user to fill in a blank in a sentence. The correct answer (from a list of correct choices) can be automatically updated if the teacher allows.
  • Recall Exercises assure you recall the facts and adapts to how the student/you learn.
  • Lets you REVIEW the material and refresh any items you've forgotten.
