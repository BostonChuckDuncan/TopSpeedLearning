Hunter Tutorials

Tutorials are being written (both written and online video) to guide new users through the steps of setting up basic and advanced applications.

Basic tutorials are fixed: in that they address all the general uses and features of Hunter.

The Advanced tutorials are a part of the licensed version of the application as purchased for a unique type, research methods, and focus on a custom Genetic-backend.

Note: These tutorials are geared toward the company/user's unique research area.
